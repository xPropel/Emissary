To Extract Images From Client
--------
Target:
To extract in game images from the League of Legends client directly.

Software needed:
- Flash Decompiler Trillix (or equivalent software that decompiles .swf files)
- League of Legends

Common images and their containing SWF files:
Mastery icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/imagePacks/ImagePack_masteryIcons.swf
Summoner spells - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/imagePacks/ImagePack_spells.swf
Item icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/imagePacks/ImagePack_items.swf
Profile icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/imagePacks/ImagePack_buddyIcons.swf

Other images NOT in SWF files:
Ability icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/images/abilities/
Champion icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/images/champions/
Champion portraits (with skins) - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/images/champions/
Rune icons - /Riot Games/League of Legends/RADS/projects/lol_air_client/releases/0.0.1.110/deploy/assets/images/runes/

Steps:
1. Open Flash Decompiler Trillix (or equivalent)
2. Identify what type images you want to extract
3. Locate the appropriate SWF file (see above) and drag it into the blank workspace of Flash Decompiler
4. In the "My tasks" window, click on "Images" and the workspace will display all the images inside the SWF file
5. Select the images you want to extract and click on "Extract objects" in the Manage ribbon
6. The program will automatically launch a window where the images are extracted
